About ResQPaws

"At RESQ Paws, we believe that every dog, no matter their ability deserves love, dignity, and a chance to live their happiest life. Founded and run by passionate students, RESQ Paws is a volunteer-driven organization dedicated to supporting dogs with disabilities. Providing mobility aids for dogs, we work to ensure that these amazing animals receive the compassion and care they deserve."

How We Do It

A simple four-step approach to empower caretakers and improve the lives of dogs with disabilities.

1

Research & Understand Needs

We begin by studying the challenges faced by disabled dogs and their caretakers — from mobility limitations to accessibility issues — ensuring our designs address real, everyday problems.

2

Design & Innovate

Our team of students and volunteers collaborates to create ergonomic, affordable, and customizable rehabilitation equipment that supports comfort, balance, and movement.

3

Build & Test

We prototype and test our equipment to ensure safety, durability, and usability. Every design is refined based on expert feedback and real-world application.

4

Support Through Technology

Through our digital platform, caretakers can access detailed guides, request custom kits, and connect with resources — making inclusive pet care simpler and more accessible.

ResQPaws Ideals

Rehabilitation Design

We develop innovative and affordable mobility aids tailored to dogs with specific disabilities, ensuring comfort, support, and ease of use.

Smart Tech Platform

Our digital platform connects caretakers with reliable information, guidance, and ready-to-use kits designed to improve animal rehabilitation.

Data-Driven Insights

We use technology and real-world feedback to continuously improve our equipment, ensuring every design is safe, efficient, and effective.

Support for Caretakers

We provide detailed tutorials, assembly guides, and expert tips to help caretakers confidently use rehabilitation equipment for their pets.

Community & Awareness

Through our platform and outreach, we aim to raise awareness about the importance of inclusive pet care and empower more people to help dogs with disabilities.
